Frequently Asked Questions

How does property sourcing work?
We assist you in discovering new investment opportunities by actively sourcing properties that align with your specific investment goals. Our goal is to find properties that maximize your potential for returns.
Is the call centre support available 24/7?
Yes, our call centre support operates 24/7 to address all inquiries promptly. Whether it's a guest concern or a property-related question, our dedicated team is here to ensure a seamless property management experience.
Why is professional photography recommended for my property?
Professional photography is essential for capturing the unique features and charm of your property. High-quality images not only attract potential clients but also contribute to effective marketing, ultimately increasing the property's market appeal.
What are the benefits of using Regal Stay for direct bookings?
Our direct booking services provide additional opportunities for increased sales and give you more control over your property. By maximising revenue potential, you can enhance the overall profitability of your investments.
